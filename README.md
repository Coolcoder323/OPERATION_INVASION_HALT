OPERATION: INVASION HALTOPERATION: INVASION HALT is a stealth and tactical social-deduction game built for the Arduboy handheld console. Inspired by Among Us, you play as a covert agent disguised aboard an alien ship headed straight for Earth. Your mission: infiltrate, sabotage, and eliminate all alien crewmates before they reach your home planet.🌌 StorylineAn alien armada is on a direct collision course with Earth. Posing as one of their own, you've snuck aboard their flagship. To save humanity, you must purge the alien crew from within using stealth, vents, and reactor sabotages—all while avoiding suspicion during emergency meetings!🕹️ ControlsActionControlMove AroundD-Pad (UP, DOWN, LEFT, RIGHT)Eliminate AlienB ButtonEnter / Exit VentA ButtonSabotage Doors / ReactorUP + A ButtonCall Meeting / Report BodyDOWN + B Button✨ Features9-Room Ship Layout: Navigate a full $3 \times 3$ grid map including the Reactor, Cafeteria, Electrical, Medbay, and more.AI Crewmates & Suspicion System: Crewmates patrol rooms, discover bodies, and vote dynamically based on your suspicious behavior.Sabotage Mechanics: Trigger door locks and reactor meltdowns to divide and conquer the crew.Customizable Settings: Toggle sound ON/OFF and adjust the Kill Cooldown (5s, 10s, 15s) to tweak game difficulty.Visual HUD Bars: Keep track of your Vent, Kill, and Meltdown Sabotage cooldowns with bottom-screen progress bars.🛠️ Hardware & Compilation RequirementsConsole: Arduboy or Arduboy FX (ATmega32u4)IDE: Arduino IDE (v1.8.x or v2.x)Required Libraries:Arduboy2ArduboyTones🚀 How to Build & FlashInstall the Arduino IDE.Install the Arduboy2 and ArduboyTones libraries via the Arduino Library Manager (Tools > Manage Libraries...).Select Arduboy as your board (Tools > Board).Open OPERATION_INVASION_HALT.ino, connect your Arduboy via USB, and click Upload.📄 LicenseThis project is open-source software licensed under the MIT License.
